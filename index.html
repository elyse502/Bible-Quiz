Welcome to BibQuiz!

This is a simple Quiz App that contains a set of bible curated questions and its answers.

Let's see how well you know your bible!

The Rules of this Quiz
1. You will have only 15 seconds for each each.
2. Once you choose your answer, you can't change it.
3. You can't select any option once the time goes off.
4. You can't exit from the Quiz while you're playing.
5. You'll get points only for questions answered correctly.
